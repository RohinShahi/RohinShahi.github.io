My Newest Project Out Now:

The Z Factor

Innovative, young, and ambitious, Generation Z is ready to make an impact on the world. But they are far different from past generations, and business leaders need to adjust their workplace practices to help this new group of workers succeed.
Drawing from experienced industry psychologists, recent studies, and personal insight, I provide a simple approach to bridging generational gaps and helping Gen Z grow and thrive.
